Lucy Tasker

Graphic Designer & Copywriter

A Snapshot:
• 10+ years of design & marketing experience
• Experience working across many industries
• Clients from start-ups to large international brands
• Facebook Certified Digital Marketing Associate

Based in the Central North Island, I specialise in all areas of graphic design, including branding, digital & print design, packaging design and illustration. I also have an extensive knowledge of digital marketing, allowing me to reach your brand goal with attention-grabbing copy and content.

I have collaborated with many established brands and organisations over the years, as well as many local independent companies and startups, in various categories, from tech, food and beverage, local independent coffee shops, craft beer brands, and street food restaurants.

I love to work with bold and passionate brands and startups that want to stand out, and be different. I believe that working collaboratively is a very important part of the branding and design process, as this gives a real insight into what makes a company different and unique.I believe that these days, It’s more important than ever to have a strong brand that helps you stand out. My bold and simplistic approach to design will help your business grow, and help it stand out in your particular sector, and marketplace.If you require a helping hand with a specific branding or design project, then please feel free to get in touch, and we can discuss your requirements.
